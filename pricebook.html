Redesign of Shure's Pricebook
Case Study


In 2023, I was given the task of redesigning the covers and inner pages of Shure's Pricebook. The Pricebook serves as an essential guidebook for dealers and sales representatives, providing them with a comprehensive product list that includes detailed descriptions and pricing information.

Before the redesign, the previous designs were created using Excel, which posed several challenges. One major issue was that these designs did not align with Shure's branding guidelines, resulting in a lack of consistency and coherence. Additionally, many of these designs were low-resolution, which affected the overall quality and professionalism of the Pricebook.

To address these issues, I embarked on creating multiple options for the covers. My primary goal was to develop a cover that would not only showcase Shure's products but also encapsulate the company's core tenets and design principles. By incorporating Shure's branding elements, such as the company's colors and logo, I aimed to create a visually appealing and cohesive design that would leave a lasting impression on the readers.

Furthermore, I made sure that the final designs for the Pricebook were reflective of Shure's overall branding strategy. By utilizing the company's colors and incorporating an ignition line that is consistent with Shure's other branding materials, I ensured that the Pricebook seamlessly integrated with Shure's visual identity.

The redesigned Pricebook now stands as a powerful tool for dealers and sales representatives, providing them with an aesthetically pleasing and informative resource. Through this redesign project, I successfully merged Shure's branding guidelines with the practicality and functionality required for an effective Pricebook.
